About ProteinPrice

Protein supplements shouldn't be confusing or expensive. ProteinPrice exists to give every athlete, gym-goer, and health-conscious person a single place to browse products and find the lowest prices — completely free.

What we're building

ProteinPrice is a free price comparison tool for protein supplements. We're building a comprehensive catalog of protein powders, bars, and drinks from the top brands — and connecting it to live pricing data from the biggest retailers.

Right now you can browse our product catalog. Live prices from major retailers are launching soon. When they do, you'll be able to instantly see where any product is cheapest.

Always free

ProteinPrice is and will always be free to use. We earn a small commission when you click through to a retailer and make a purchase — this doesn't change the price you pay.

We don't accept paid placements, we don't show ads, and we never sell your data.

How it works

Simple, transparent, and free

1

Our team builds and maintains a comprehensive catalog of protein products from hundreds of brands, including nutritional data, flavors, and sizes.

2

3

Affiliate disclosure

ProteinPrice participates in affiliate programs. When you click a link to a retailer and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. This helps us keep the site free. We only link to legitimate retailers and our price data is not influenced by commercial relationships.

Privacy policy

We collect only the information necessary to provide our service. If you set a price alert, we store your email address to send you notifications. We do not sell, share, or rent your personal data to third parties.

We use anonymous analytics to understand how the site is used and improve it. You can unsubscribe from price alerts at any time by contacting us or using the manage alerts feature.

Terms of use

ProteinPrice provides product and pricing information for informational purposes only. Prices and availability are subject to change. Always confirm the final price at the retailer before purchasing.
